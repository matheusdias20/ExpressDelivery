How it works

Within the app, the customer will request a delivery and a delivery person will accept it.

With the order accepted, ExpressDelivery sends a text message or e-mail with the shipment data and the customer can track the product, from the stock to the delivery path.

In addition to personal deliveries, the company works with offices, e-commerces and restaurants.
